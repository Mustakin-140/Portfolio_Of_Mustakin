Welcome to My School Life

My school was Shaheed Police Smrity College. It is situated in Mirpur, Dhaka. I started my schooling in 2007. I studied here from Class 1 and finished my school journey in 2017 when I gave my SSC exam. So it was a 10 years long journey.

I made so many friends in my school life. We had some good and memorable moments with them. Even though we are now in different places, still I can remember these memories. In fact school life was the sweetest time of my life.
