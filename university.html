Welcome to My University Life

I took admission at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering of Southeast University in the Spring 2020 semester. I made a circle of people within a short time. But after the Mid term exam of 1st semester, the campus was shut down due to Covid-19 virus. So we started our university life virtually with online class, exam, presentation, viva.

After 2 years, the on campus class started in Summer 2022 semester. We went back to the campus in a new normal life. Everything started in a new way. In January 2023, our university was shifted to the dreamy permanent campus at Tejgaon, at the heart of the Dhaka city. I completed my researh work under the supervision of Roksana Akter Madam, Assistant Professor, Department of CSE, Southeast University. My other team members were: Mohammad Shafiqur Rahman and Israt Jahan Juhi.

I joined as Intern at the Office of the Information Technology (IT) of Southeast University on 25 February, 2024. Here I experienced so many things. I worked with seniors from my department. It was a nice, friendly environment. I worked as a Software Tester of the University Management System (UMS) of Southeasy University (SEU). I also did data collection, data entry, data formatting and data validation under the Supply Chain Management and Inventory System Project of the university. Overall it was a great experience of working here. My internship period ended on 30 June, 2024. My final result was published on 09 July, 2024 and then I became a graduate of this university.

University life gave me lots of good memories and also tough times. My respected teachers were very helpful and motivated me with their valuable advices. I met some seniors who never behaved like a senior but like a friend. I also found some good and potential juniors who can do well in future. I had some good and helpful batchmates and friends. I was involved with various extra curricular activities. I was the Vice President and President of the Southeast Computer Club in 2022 and 2023 respectively. I have learned event management, time management, team work, leadership, communication skills. I found a good community here. So this is all about my university life.
