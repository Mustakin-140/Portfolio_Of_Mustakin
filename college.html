Welcome to My College Life

My collage name is Shaheed Police Smrity College. It is situated in Mirpur, Dhaka. I got admitted here in 2017 after passing SSC. I found some guys who were also from my SSC batch. I college life was not that much colorful. After my admission, my father became sick and hospitalized. During this tough time, I had to struggle a lot to continue my study. But by the Grace of Allah, I stood 10th position among all the boys and girls of Science group at the Half yearly exam. That was really unexpected from me. But after 8 days of result publishing, my father left us forever. It was a shocking event for me as we lost umbrella over us.

After the departure of my father, I came back again to my study. I regained myself and focused on my life. Then HSC exam appeared. I sat for the exam and achieved GPA-4.75. Even though it was not a good grade point considering the whole country's result but still it was satisfactory for me and my family. Then I started to take my preparation for the public university admission test. I did not do too bad there. But due to low GPA I could not get good position for desired subjects. Thus I was unable to get admission in any public university. In this part, I have discussed my college life along with the admission test journey.
